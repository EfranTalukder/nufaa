Staking explained

Your money earns
while it sits.

When you deposit money into your Nufaa wallet, we acquire Sharia-compliant digital assets on your behalf. These assets are staked on secure blockchain networks — Solana, Cardano, Ethereum — which generate real cryptographic rewards.

Those rewards are converted and credited to your wallet automatically. You own the assets. You earn the yields. We take a small operational fee — never interest.
