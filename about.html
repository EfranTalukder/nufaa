Our story

Engineering ethical
finance for Gambia.

We exist to prove that technology can serve communities — not exploit them. Nufaa is a Sharia-compliant rewards marketplace where your money grows through staking, not shrinks through interest.

The founder

Built by someone who
lived the problem.

Alhassan Bah grew up watching families across Gambia trapped in cycles of high-interest debt just to keep the lights on or buy rice for the week. The banking system was designed to extract wealth from communities, not create it.

He saw an opportunity where blockchain staking yields could replace interest charges entirely — creating a financial system where paying for everyday essentials actually builds wealth instead of destroying it.

Nufaa is the result: a platform where fairness is not aspirational, but operational. Where every transaction follows Islamic finance principles. Where your money works for you from the moment you deposit it.

The mission

We are building a rewards marketplace that breaks the cycle of predatory banking in Gambia. Buy electricity, airtime, rice, and household essentials — all interest-free, all powered by blockchain staking rewards.

Every Dalasi deposited into Nufaa acquires Sharia-compliant digital assets that earn real yields. Those yields pay down your balance or grow your wallet. The community grows together.

Why Sharia-compliant?

In Islamic finance, charging interest (Riba) is prohibited because it exploits vulnerability. Conventional banking systems thrive by taxing the poorest populations with the highest rates.

Nufaa uses a Wakala (agency) structure. When you buy through us, you are not taking a loan. The blockchain staking yields the economic value to settle the transaction. Finance grounded in fairness, transparency, and shared prosperity.

Vision & roadmap

We are building in phases — each one expanding what Gambians can access interest-free.

  • Phase 1: Utilities & essentials

    Nawec electricity, Africell airtime, rice, cooking oil, and household goods — available now through our agent network.

  • Phase 2: Full marketplace

    Expanding to clothing, electronics, school supplies, and more — all purchasable through Nufaa with staking-backed payments.

  • Phase 3: SME financing

    Allowing small businesses to acquire tools, machinery, and inventory interest-free — funded by staking rewards.
