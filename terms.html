Nufaa operates strictly on a Sharia-compliant Wakala (agency) model. By using our services to pay for utilities (such as Nawec or Africell), you appoint Nufaa as your agent to manage the underlying digital assets. You acknowledge that no interest (Riba) is charged or accumulated on your account.

3. Staking & Asset Management

Your service cost corresponds to an equivalent acquisition of Sharia-compliant digital assets. Nufaa stakes these assets on enterprise blockchains to generate yields. These yields automatically pay down your balance. You bear no risk of the balance increasing due to interest or penalties.
